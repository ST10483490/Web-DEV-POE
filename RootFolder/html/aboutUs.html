Our History

Charly’s Bakery, located in District Six, Cape Town, was founded in 1989 by Charly and Jacqui Biess and is now run by their daughters. Known for its vibrant creativity and whimsical spirit, the bakery specializes in cakes for all occasions. From humble beginnings at Thibault Square to fame at the V&A Waterfront, Charly’s has grown into a beloved family-run institution. In 2009, the bakery moved into Cape Town’s old Jewish bookstore, embracing bold traditions and sharing its story worldwide through Charly’s Cake Angels.

MISION

  • Deliver freshly baked goods crafted daily from locally sourced ingredients.
  • Create a welcoming community space where people connect and feel at home.
  • Blend tradition with innovation, honouring classic baking while introducing creative Flavors.

VISION

  • Become the most trusted and beloved bakery in the region.
  • Spread joy and togetherness through every cake, pastry, and loaf.
